About Bay Plumbing

Local Experts Who've Seen It All

Founded in 2006 by master plumber Gary Nguyen, Bay Plumbing Co has grown from a one-truck operation into the Bay Area's most trusted plumbing team. Every technician on our crew is licensed, background-checked, and trained on the latest systems — from classic copper pipe to modern PEX and smart leak detectors.

We built our reputation on one simple rule: fix it right the first time. That's why over 60% of our business comes from repeat customers and their referrals.

All Work Fully Warranted
